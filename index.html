THE STORY

Hey, I'm Tana. I'm an artist who believes that a simple hat can change the world—or at least, our little corner of it in Ferndale.

I can't donate thousands of dollars, but I can paint. And I believe that if we start talking to each other again, we can stop the hate and find our community.

"Finding common ground is the first step toward a better world."

Every hat I make is an invitation to listen, to learn about civics, to understand our local elections, and to care for our neighbors.
