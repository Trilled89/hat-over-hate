Discover the Mission

THE VISION

Cultivating connection through creative expression.

I'm Tana, an artist dedicated to rebuilding the social fabric of our local community. I believe that sustainable change starts with a single, respectful conversation. While I may not have thousands to donate, I have a brush and a belief: that when we lead with curiosity instead of judgment, we find our common ground.

Every hat is a portable bridge—an invitation to discuss civics, care for neighbors, and actively participate in our local democracy.
