Supporting Whatcom County one hand-painted conversation at a time.

The Story

Hey, I'm Tana. I'm an artist in Ferndale, and I believe that if we can just find community and care about each other, we can stop the polarization.

I can't donate money to the movements already doing good work in Whatcom County, but I can paint. I'm not trying to start a movement—I'm trying to be a catalyst for the ones already running.

“It's about going out, having those difficult conversations, and being the one who listens.”
Philosophy

Stubbornness vs. Listening

People are so divided now. I enjoy hearing both sides because I know beliefs have a source—families, grandmas, history.

Realizing the Truth

I had to realize things on my own, not because someone taught me, but because someone listened to me.

Collection

Ferndale Focus

Inspired by the local landscape and the bridges we build.

The Listener

Support

I only need six hats to make an impression. To keep painting and connecting, I need to source hats and paint. Paint is expensive, and while I have some, I need finances to keep this catalyst running.

Every dollar goes directly into materials for the community.

How to Help
